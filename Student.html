Job Attributes Overview

Retail

Retail jobs involve customer service, sales, and inventory management. Employees often work in stores or online platforms.

Tutoring

Tutors help students with various subjects, offering personalized learning experiences to improve academic performance.

Sales

Sales roles focus on selling products or services. Strong communication and persuasion skills are key in these positions.

Freelancing

Freelancers offer specialized services, such as writing, design, or development, on a contract basis with flexibility.

Delivery Driver

Delivery drivers transport goods to various locations, ensuring timely and safe deliveries. Good knowledge of routes is essential.

Fitness Instructor

Fitness instructors guide individuals or groups through workouts, helping clients achieve their fitness goals with motivation and expertise.

Housekeeping

Housekeepers maintain cleanliness and order in homes, hotels, or businesses, requiring attention to detail and organizational skills.

Hospitality

Hospitality roles include managing guest services in hotels, restaurants, and events, focusing on customer satisfaction and service quality.

Finance

Finance professionals manage money, investments, and financial planning, using analytical skills to guide individuals and businesses.
