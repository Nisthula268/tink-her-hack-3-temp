Job Details

ABC Gym is looking for a dedicated and passionate fitness instructor to join our team. As a fitness instructor, you will lead group classes, provide one-on-one training, and help our members achieve their personal fitness goals. You should be energetic, motivating, and have a strong passion for fitness. This is an exciting opportunity to make a real difference in people's lives while growing your career in the fitness industry!

Job Requirements

  • Certified fitness instructor (e.g., NASM, ACE, or equivalent certification)
  • Strong knowledge of fitness and exercise techniques
  • Ability to lead and motivate individuals and groups
  • Excellent communication and interpersonal skills
  • Ability to work flexible hours, including evenings and weekends
  • Prior experience in fitness instruction is a plus, but not required

Salary

Starting from $25 per hour + bonuses for performance

Fitness instructors are paid an hourly wage, with the potential to earn additional income based on performance and client feedback. The salary may vary depending on experience and class attendance.

Benefits

  • Competitive hourly wage plus performance-based bonuses
  • Flexible work schedule
  • Free gym membership
  • Opportunity for career growth and certification support
  • Employee discounts on fitness gear and products
