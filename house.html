Job Details

XYZ Hotel is looking for dedicated housekeeping staff to join our team. As a housekeeper, you will be responsible for maintaining the cleanliness and appearance of guest rooms, common areas, and facilities. Your tasks will include cleaning, restocking supplies, and ensuring that guests have a pleasant and comfortable stay. We are looking for reliable, detail-oriented individuals who take pride in their work and enjoy providing exceptional service.

Job Requirements

  • Prior experience in housekeeping or cleaning roles is preferred but not required
  • Ability to perform physical tasks such as bending, lifting, and standing for extended periods
  • Attention to detail and commitment to cleanliness
  • Strong organizational skills
  • Ability to work efficiently and independently
  • Good communication skills and ability to work as part of a team
  • Flexible schedule, including weekends and holidays

Salary

Starting from $18 per hour

Housekeepers are paid an hourly wage, with the potential to earn additional income from tips. The salary may vary depending on experience and shift schedules.

Benefits

  • Competitive hourly wage
  • Flexible work schedule
  • Free meals during shifts
  • Employee discounts on hotel services
  • Opportunities for career advancement within the company
  • Health and wellness benefits (for full-time employees)
